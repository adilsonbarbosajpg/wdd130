Rio Grande do Sul

Rio Grande do Sul is a Brazilian state that is part of the Southern region, being the southernmost unit of the national territory. The state government headquarters is located in the city of Porto Alegre. With 10.8 million inhabitants, the population of Rio Grande do Sul is the sixth largest in Brazil.
